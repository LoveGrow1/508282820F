Action recommendations

Open Discussions on Morality:

Initiate conversations about your individual beliefs and values, find similarities, and respect differences. This could lead to a deeper understanding of each other and create a stronger bond.

Personal Space Conversations:

Talk about what 'personal space' means to each of you. Be open and discuss your needs without blaming or becoming defensive. This can lead to a balanced solution that ensures both your needs are met.

Family Time and Alone Time:

Given your history together and your shared child, remember to schedule time for the three of you, as well as individual moments to recharge. Appreciate that having time for personal reflection is important and healthy for the relationship.
